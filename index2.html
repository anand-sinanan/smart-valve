How it Works

Outline separately with images :

1. Buy device

Either online or coming soon to stores

2. Register device

Set up to protect and remotely control your device, enjoy rich, free feature sets such as reports + device statistics

3. That's it!

Seriously, you're done! By default SmartValve will automatically attempt to water your lawn based on weather conditions and environmental factors so that you can sit back and let us do all the work.
